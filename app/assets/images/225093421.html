Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Ruby on Rails (RoR) developer, to work alongside an existing contractor in an agile, test-driven development environment.

The successful applicant will need to evidence their experience in the following areas:

- Rails development (including Rails 3.1)
- jQuery
- Git / Github
- TDD (Test-Driven Development)
- Excellent English language skills

I am looking for someone who is available 8-10 hours or more per week - any timezone is OK. If you work Saturday / Sunday instead of weekdays, that is even better.

As this project is test-driven, the successful applicant will need to have excellent English language skills, and be able to translate user stories into working tests, and then into working code.

Prospective applicants will be required to complete a short test task (approx. 30 mins - 1 hour), which will be paid at a rate of $10/hr upon successful completion.

NOTE: Any cover letters that refer to skills not mentioned in the list above will be assumed to be recycled, and will be rejected as spam, without consideration. Also, I am looking for independent contractors only.

Please do not apply with rates higher than $13.33 per hour (though preferably lower) as I do not have a budget for anything higher than this. Please keep in mind that the lower the rate, the more work will be allocated to you.
